Section II: Object of the contract

II.1) Description
II.1.1) Title attributed to the contract by the contracting authority:
EGNOS GEO satellite transponder service replenishment.
II.1.2) Type of contract and location of works, place of delivery or of performance:
Services.
Service category: No 27.
II.1.3) The notice involves:
A public contract.
II.1.5) Short description of the contract or purchase(s):
The object of the contract is the procurement of a service which contemplates 2 main elements:
? the procurement and the operations service of the EGNOS geostationary (GEO) transponder payloads, operating in C or Ku-band for the up-link and L-band for the down-link, namely due by early 2011 (GEO 1 = lot 1) and mid 2012 (GEO 2 = lot 2);
? the hosting and operations service of the 2 navigation land earth stations associated with the GEO transponders (including adaptation of the radio-frequency sections to uplink the signals to the satellite).
Contractual obligations are assumed up to 31.1.2014.

35320000.
