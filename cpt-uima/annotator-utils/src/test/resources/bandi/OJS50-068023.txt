The Commission needs a dataset to update an internal database on scheduled flights (may include trains, buses, lorries, ferries used/marketed for similar purpose as aircraft, i.e. carrying passengers and/or freight) for the last half of 2008, years 2009 and 2010, and first half of 2011 (cfr. specifications ? Section I.2.1).

Lot No 2

Title: A service for aircraft fleets
1) Short description:
The Commission needs a PC-based multi-user solution that holds information on airline fleets, accidents and company contact information from not later than 29.9.2008 onwards. The service should enable customised queries and data exports from the database (cfr. specifications ? Section I.2.2).
2) Common procurement vocabulary (CPV):
73200000.
3) Quantity or scope:
Estimated duration: 3 × 12 months.
Estimated cost excluding VAT: EUR 50 000.
5) Additional information about lots:

Lot No 3

Title: Web-based service related to air transport industry
1) Short description:
The Commission is interested in a subscription (for a minimum of 3 users) to a web-based service that allows us to monitor the state of the air transport industry from not later than 21.11.2008 onwards (cfr. specifications ? Section I.2.3).

73200000.

Estimated duration: 3 × 12 months.

Lot No 4

Title: A service to provide analyses on fares
1) Short description:
The Commission wants to focus on the worldwide development on actual issued-ticket fares. As a first step the intention is as a minimum to get an overview of the biggest routes (by passengers) between, by and within regions (cfr. specifications ? Section I.2.4).
