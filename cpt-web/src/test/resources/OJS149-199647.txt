II.1.3) The notice involves:

II.1.5) Short description of the contract or purchase(s):
The objective of the study is to determine the maximum extent to which ethanol, blended directly into base petrol at 5 % v/v, 10 % v/v and 25 % v/v, has on the volatility parameters included in the European petrol standard EN228 (using the established test methods). In other words, it will measure the volatility parameter of the base petrol and then those of the 3 ethanol blends. It will be necessary to consider both winter and summer petrols. The study will focus on those petrols that are considered likely to generate the biggest variations in the results.
